3,000 footers

Mount Chocorua

Mount Chocorua's elevation is 3,478 feet. It is located in Albany, New Hampshire. While this mountain is not a 4,000 footer, it is still a beautiful hike that has a breathtaking view from the top. There are a few different trails to get to the summit. Two of the more popular trails are the Piper trail and the Carterledge trail.

Mount Hitchcock

Mount Hitchcock is located in Lincoln, New Hampshire. It's elevation is 3,620 feet. This mountain towers over the Kancamagus highway. However, the peek of this mountain is wooded which provides only a few views. This mountain is one of the top one hundred highest mountains in New Hampshire.

Sandwich Mountain

Sandwich mountain is located just south of the Waterville Valley. This mountains elevation is 3,980 feet just 20 feet short of being one of the many 4,000 footers in New Hampshire. The top of the mountain has great views. This mountain would be more popular amoung man hikers if it was 2o feet taller because it has a well maintained trail thats very easy to access.

Mount Nancy

Mount Nancy is located in Lincoln New Hampshire with an elevation of 3,926 feet. This mountain starts off as an easy hike but once you get further in the trail becomes very steep before leveling off to the peek of the mountain. Another obsticle in hiking this mountain is that it does not have a maintained trail to the top.
