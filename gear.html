A good pair of sneakers or hiking boots are an essential part of hiking. They make hiking up steap parts of the mountain much easier. As well as protecting the feet from anything nature will throw at you. Some good brands of hiking boots are:

  • Merrell
  • Salomon
  • Scarpa

Hydration Bladder

A hydration bladder while not essential is also a very good piece of hiking gear to consider because it makes carrying water easier. This is also important to consider because you don't waste water bottles and most backpacks have a seperate spot to put it to make it convient. Some good inexpensive Brands are:

  • CamelBak
  • Hydrapak
  • Osprey

Backpack

A good hiking bag makes everything more enjoyable. It makes holding all of your gear easy and comftorable. It is important to make sure the backpack is adjusted properly to have the majority of the weight on the shoulders. Before purchasing the bag make sure to cunsider the size and whether or not is the right size for you. There are a few different types to consider:

  • Overnight Pack
  • Day trip
  • Short distance Pack

Snacks

You can never bring enough snacks when you go hiking. You never know what will happen also snacks are a good way to get energy and keep yourself motivated on a hard hike. Healthy snacks are good for you and also taste great. Some healthy and deliciuos snacks to consider bringing with you are:

  • Clif bars
